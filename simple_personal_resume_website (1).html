My Journey

My interest in technology started early. While in high school, I attended a trade school program focused on Information Security, where I built a strong foundation in computer and security fundamentals. During this time, I earned the MTA Security Fundamentals certification in 2021.

Outside of school, I have been building and working on computers for years. What began as curiosity grew into a desire to deeply understand how hardware, operating systems, and networks function together.

That passion carried into my professional career. I earned my current role as a Distribution Center IT Technician by networking internally, demonstrating my enthusiasm for technology, and showing a genuine desire to understand how systems interact in real-world environments. This hands-on experience continues to push me toward a long-term career in cybersecurity.
